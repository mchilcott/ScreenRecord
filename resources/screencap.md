# Online screen capture

There are a number of screen capture tools, which use your browser to capture the screen, your microphone, and optionally, your webcam.

- To do this, select one of the services below, and follow the instructions.
- Note that it may take some time after you finish your recording for the video to be available, so please be patient.

- [https://recordscreen.io/](https://recordscreen.io/)
- [https://www.panopto.com/record/](https://www.panopto.com/record/)
